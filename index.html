Project

Overview

My project will be an isometric tower defense game called Gods Vs Titans. The aim of the game is to defend your base from the encroaching Titans by building powerful Gods and synergising them with their respective demi-gods. The game will include a campaign that can be played solo or with friends. Gods Vs Titans is a complex and challenging game, pulling mechanics from the recently popular Dota auto chess/ Team fight Tactics and Bloons Tower Defense 6.

Motivation

The motivation stems from wanting to create something fun, unique and challenging that is my own. The success of Bloons Tower Defense and Team Fight Tactics indicates a desire for these types of genres… so why not combine the two. In order to get a foothold into the gaming industry I must build up my portfolio.

Description

Tower System

  • Gods and Demi-gods are towers
  • Towers can equip items and have spells
  • They have primary attributes; Agility, Intellect, Strength
  • Towers and enemies both have health and mana
  • Towers auto attack and cast spells
  • Only 3 Gods and 8 Demi-gods can be placed on the map at once  

Scroll System

  • Players can roll for new heroes through common, uncommon and rare rolls that progressively have a higher change of giving better heroes
  • Gods and demi-gods have their own rarity: Normal, Rare, Super Rare and Super Super Rare

Star System

  • Towers, Items and Spells all have their own star level
  • Towers need to have a star level equal or higher than the star level of the item or spell in order to equip it 
  • The Towers star level can be increased by finding more of the same unit from scrolls
  • Items get ridiculously strong the higher their star level; incentivizing the user to level up the Gods  

Demi-god System

  • Demi-gods are lesser than normal Gods but can empower both to become greater than the sum of their parts  
  • Each God gets randomly assigned a Demi-god to synergize with  

Enemy System

  • Enemies walk down a lane. The lane loops so the amount of enemies grows infinitely until they are killed
  • Normal enemies have a value of 1. Bosses have a value of 5. If the total value of enemies on screen exceeds 100 you lose the game
  • They have spells and abilites that can cause elusiveness and negative status effects towards towers
  • Enemies drop gold and chests

Item System

  • You buy items with through the store with Gold
  • Items can be combined with other items to create something stronger
  • Items can have usable abilities like stun or slow
  • A tower can only hold 6 items at a time
  • Stronger Items have a higher Star level and require more gold
  • You can obtain items by opening chests with their respective keys
  • Items increase a towers primary attributes
